Faishal (pronounced: fæɪ-ʃæl) is a fresh graduate majoring in Telecommunication Engineering at Institut Teknologi Bandung (ITB). His research interests span Networked Systems, Computer Networks, Systems for ML, Multimedia Systems and Edge Computing. He believes these topics will play an important role in the future. Also, He wants to be an innovator in this fast-paced change and modern world; an era in which disruptive technologies rewrite history.
